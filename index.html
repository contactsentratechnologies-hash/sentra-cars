The Process

How It Works — Step by Step

We've made the Japan car import process as simple as buying locally. Here's exactly what happens, who does what, and when.

01
🔍
You Choose Your Car
Tell us your budget, preferred model, and must-have features. We'll find matching vehicles from verified Japan auctions and export platforms. You see the actual auction photos and grade reports.
We Do This
02
🤝
Price Agreement
Once you choose a vehicle, we confirm the FOB price with the Japanese exporter. You receive a full breakdown: vehicle price, shipping cost, insurance estimate, and our 2% commission — before committing.
We Do This
03
🏦
Your Bank Opens an LC
You visit your Sri Lankan bank and open a Letter of Credit (LC) in favour of the Japanese exporter. The bank controls the payment — the money only releases when shipping documents confirm the car was sent.
You + Your Bank
04
🔒
Bank Guarantees Payment
The LC is a legally binding bank guarantee. The exporter in Japan is guaranteed payment ONLY after they ship the vehicle and submit proper documents (Bill of Lading, invoice, inspection certificate).
Your Bank Handles
05
🚗
Vehicle Prepared in Japan
The exporter inspects and prepares the vehicle for export. We verify the inspection report and pre-shipment photos on your behalf. The car is booked on the next available RoRo or container vessel.
We Verify & Coordinate
06
🚢
Vehicle Ships from Japan
The car sets sail from Japan (typically Nagoya, Osaka, or Yokohama port) to Colombo Port. Transit takes approximately 16–27 days. You receive the Bill of Lading reference to track the shipment.
We Track & Update You
07
📄
Documents to Sri Lanka
Shipping documents (Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Insurance Certificate) are sent to your Sri Lankan bank. The bank releases payment to the exporter upon receiving valid documents.
Your Bank Handles
08
🛃
Customs Clearance
Once the car arrives at Colombo Port, we coordinate with a licensed Customs House Agent (CHA) to handle the declaration and clearance. You pay Customs Duty, Excise Duty, VAT, and any applicable Luxury Tax directly to Sri Lanka Customs. Clearance typically takes 7–14 days.
We Coordinate
09
📋
DMT Registration
After customs clearance, we handle the Department of Motor Traffic (DMT) registration at Werahera or your nearest regional office. You receive your official registration certificate and number plate within 1–2 weeks. Third-party insurance is arranged.
We Handle Paperwork
10
🏠
Delivered to Your Door
Your fully registered, road-legal vehicle is delivered directly to your home or any address in Sri Lanka. You receive all original documents: registration certificate, customs clearance, invoice, and warranty information.
We Deliver

Trust & Legal

How We Protect You

Importing a car is a major financial decision. Here's the legal and operational framework that protects every buyer.

🏛️
Registered Business
Sentra is a legally registered business in Sri Lanka. Our registration documents are available for inspection upon request before you commit to anything.
📜
Client Agreement Contract
Before we begin, you sign a formal client agreement that details our exact commission, your rights, the process timeline, and our responsibilities. No verbal promises — everything is in writing.
🏦
Letter of Credit Protection
Your payment is protected by your Sri Lankan bank's Letter of Credit. The Japanese exporter only receives funds after presenting genuine shipping documents. Your bank is the gatekeeper — not us.
🔍
Verified Japanese Exporters
We work only with licensed, reputable Japanese exporters who have a track record of Sri Lanka shipments. We conduct pre-shipment vehicle history checks using Japanese auction grade reports.
📋
Full Invoice Transparency
You receive copies of every document: Japan invoice, shipping bill, customs declaration, duty payment receipts. Nothing is hidden. You can verify every single payment independently.
🛃
Licensed CHA Partner
Our customs clearance is handled by a licensed Customs House Agent (CHA) registered with Sri Lanka Customs. Duties are calculated and paid transparently — you receive official duty payment receipts.
💬
WhatsApp Updates at Every Step
You're never left in the dark. We send you updates via WhatsApp at every milestone: vehicle confirmed, LC opened, car shipped, arrived at port, customs cleared, registration done, delivery booked.
🚫
Zero Hidden Charges — Guaranteed
We sign a written guarantee that our only charge is our 2% commission. If any undisclosed charge appears, you have the right to terminate the agreement and receive a full refund of our commission.
